WHAT WILL BREAK AT SCALE 🔴
The holdings data problem is the biggest risk. Your entire value proposition — overlap detection, sector exposure, health scoring — depends on holdings data. Right now you have:

10 funds seeded
3–9 holdings per fund (real funds have 50–100 stocks)
Hardcoded codes that may or may not match real AMFI codes
No date filtering (will double-count when monthly updates arrive)
No scheduled job to fetch AMFI's monthly portfolio disclosures

This means: when a user adds "Mirae Asset Large Cap" and "Parag Parikh Flexi Cap" expecting to see overlap, if either fund's scheme code isn't in your seeded list, they see zero overlap. Not "partial overlap" — zero. This is a silent correctness failure.
